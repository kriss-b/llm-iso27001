# Mobile Device and Teleworking Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes guidelines for the secure use of **mobile devices** and **teleworking** to protect information assets and ensure compliance with **ISO 27001:2022 (Controls A.8.1 — User endpoint devices, and A.6.7 — Remote working)**.

---

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, and third-party vendors.
- All mobile devices (e.g., laptops, smartphones, tablets) used to access Company systems or data.
- All teleworking arrangements, including remote work and work-from-home.

---

## 3. Policy Statements
### 3.1 Mobile Device Security
- **Device Encryption**: All mobile devices shall be encrypted (e.g., BitLocker, FileVault).
- **Authentication**: Devices shall be protected with strong passwords, PINs, or biometric authentication.
- **Lost or Stolen Devices**: Report lost or stolen devices to the IT Team **immediately**.
- **Software Updates**: Install security updates and patches within **7 days** of release.
- **Prohibited Actions**:
  - Rooting or jailbreaking devices.
  - Installing unauthorized software.
  - Connecting to unsecured public Wi-Fi networks without a VPN.

### 3.2 Teleworking Security
- **Secure Connection**: Use a **Company-approved VPN** to access internal systems.
- **Work Environment**: Ensure a secure and private workspace (e.g., locked room, no unauthorized access).
- **Data Protection**: Store sensitive data only on Company-approved systems (e.g., cloud storage, encrypted drives).
- **Printing**: Avoid printing sensitive documents at home. If necessary, securely dispose of printed materials.

### 3.3 Bring Your Own Device (BYOD)
- **BYOD Registration**: Employees shall register personal devices with the IT Team before accessing Company systems.
- **Company Rights**: The Company reserves the right to wipe Company data from personal devices if lost or stolen.
- **Separation of Data**: Use separate profiles or containers for personal and Company data.

### 3.4 Monitoring and Compliance
- The Company reserves the right to monitor mobile devices and teleworking activities for security purposes.
- Non-compliance with this policy may result in revocation of mobile device or teleworking privileges.

---

## 4. Compliance
Non-compliance with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contracts.
