# Asset Management Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy establishes the requirements for managing organizational assets to ensure their protection, appropriate usage, and compliance with **ISO 27001:2022 (Controls A.5.9 — Inventory of information and other associated assets, and A.5.12 — Classification of information)**.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all assets owned or used by the organization, including:
- Physical assets (e.g., laptops, servers, networking equipment).
- Software assets (e.g., applications, licenses).
- Information assets (e.g., data, documents, intellectual property).
- Digital services (e.g., web applications, email accounts, backup systems).

## 3. Policy Statements
### 3.1 Inventory of Assets
Maintain an up-to-date inventory of all assets, including their classification, location, and assigned owner.

### 3.2 Ownership and Classification
Every asset shall have an assigned owner responsible for its management, security, and classification based on sensitivity and criticality.

### 3.3 Acceptable Use
Define and communicate acceptable use guidelines for all asset categories. Employees shall be trained on these guidelines.

### 3.4 Protection of Assets
Implement controls to protect assets against unauthorized access, destruction, modification, or interference.

### 3.5 Disposal of Assets
Dispose of assets securely and in compliance with applicable regulations and organizational policies.

## 4. Compliance
Non-compliance with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contracts.
