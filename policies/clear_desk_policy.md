# Clear Desk and Clear Screen Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy ensures the protection of sensitive information by establishing guidelines for maintaining a **clear desk** and **clear screen**. This policy aligns with **ISO 27001:2022 (Control A.7.7)** and reduces the risk of unauthorized access, loss, or theft of information.

---

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, and third-party vendors.
- All physical and digital workspaces, including:
  - Offices, desks, and workstations.
  - Computers, laptops, and mobile devices.
  - Printed documents and physical media.

---

## 3. Policy Statements
### 3.1 Clear Desk
- **Physical Documents**: Store sensitive documents in locked drawers or cabinets when not in use.
- **Physical Media**: Secure USB drives, external hard drives, and other media in locked storage.
- **Work Areas**: Keep work areas tidy and free of sensitive information when unattended.

### 3.2 Clear Screen
- **Lock Workstations**: Lock computer screens when leaving workstations unattended (e.g., `Win + L` or `Ctrl + Cmd + Q`).
- **Timeout Settings**: Enable automatic screen lock after **5 minutes** of inactivity.
- **Mobile Devices**: Enable password, PIN, or biometric authentication for mobile devices.

### 3.3 Printing and Scanning
- **Secure Printing**: Use secure print release features (e.g., badge authentication) for sensitive documents.
- **Retrieve Documents**: Retrieve printed documents immediately from printers and scanners.
- **Secure Disposal**: Shred or securely dispose of sensitive documents when no longer needed.

### 3.4 Remote Work
- **Secure Workspace**: Ensure remote workspaces are secure and free from unauthorized access.
- **Virtual Backgrounds**: Use virtual backgrounds or blur features during video calls to prevent exposure of sensitive information.

---

## 4. Compliance
Non-compliance with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contracts.
