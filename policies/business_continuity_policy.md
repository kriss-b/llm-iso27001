# Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery Policy

## 1. Purpose
This policy ensures the resilience of **ACME CORP** by establishing a framework for business continuity and disaster recovery (BC/DR) in alignment with **ISO 27001:2022 (Control A.5.29)**. The goal is to minimize disruption to business operations and protect critical assets during unforeseen events.

---

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to:
- All employees, contractors, and third-party vendors.
- All critical business processes, systems, and data.
- All locations where business operations are conducted.

---

## 3. Policy Statements
### 3.1 Business Impact Analysis (BIA)
- Conduct a **Business Impact Analysis (BIA)** annually to identify critical business processes and their recovery priorities.
- Define **Recovery Time Objectives (RTO)** and **Recovery Point Objectives (RPO)** for each critical process.

### 3.2 Business Continuity Plan (BCP)
- Develop and maintain a **Business Continuity Plan (BCP)** that includes:
  - Roles and responsibilities during a disruption.
  - Communication protocols for internal and external stakeholders.
  - Procedures for maintaining critical operations.
  - Recovery strategies for IT systems, data, and facilities.

### 3.3 Disaster Recovery Plan (DRP)
- Develop and maintain a **Disaster Recovery Plan (DRP)** for IT systems and data, including:
  - Backup and restoration procedures.
  - Alternate processing sites (e.g., cloud, colocation).
  - Failover and redundancy mechanisms.

### 3.4 Testing and Exercises
- Conduct **annual tests** of the BCP and DRP to ensure effectiveness.
- Document test results and update plans based on findings.

### 3.5 Training and Awareness
- Provide **annual training** to employees on their roles during a disruption.
- Conduct **awareness campaigns** to ensure preparedness.

### 3.6 Third-Party Continuity
- Ensure third-party vendors have BC/DR plans that align with this policy.
- Include BC/DR requirements in vendor contracts.

---

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities
### 4.1 COO (Owner)
- Oversee the development and implementation of the BCP and DRP.
- Ensure alignment with business objectives and ISO 27001:2022.

### 4.2 IT Team
- Develop and maintain the DRP.
- Implement and test backup and recovery procedures.

### 4.3 Department Heads
- Identify critical processes and dependencies for the BIA.
- Ensure employees are trained and aware of their roles during a disruption.

### 4.4 Employees
- Participate in BC/DR training and exercises.
- Follow procedures outlined in the BCP and DRP during a disruption.

---

## 5. Compliance
Non-compliance with this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contracts.

---

## 6. Review and Updates
This policy will be reviewed **annually** or after significant changes to business processes or threats.
